## üåü Our Standards

### Positive Behavior (‚úÖ):

1. **Professional Communication**:
   - Using welcoming and inclusive language
   - Being respectful of differing viewpoints
   - Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
   - Focusing on what's best for the community

2. **Collaboration**:
   - Helping others learn and grow
   - Sharing knowledge generously
   - Giving credit where credit is due
   - Contributing constructively

3. **Respect for IP**:
   - Acknowledging Faheemly's intellectual property rights
   - Not attempting to copy or replicate the platform
   - Directing others to official Faheemly resources
   - Respecting licensing and copyright

4. **Responsible Disclosure**:
   - Reporting security vulnerabilities privately
   - Not exploiting vulnerabilities maliciously
   - Following responsible disclosure guidelines

### Unacceptable Behavior (‚ùå):

1. **Harassment**:
   - Offensive comments related to protected characteristics
   - Personal attacks or insults
   - Trolling or inflammatory comments
   - Unwelcome sexual attention or advances

2. **Unprofessional Conduct**:
   - Publishing others' private information without permission
   - Sustained disruption of discussions
   - Spamming or excessive self-promotion
   - Threatening or intimidating behavior

3. **Intellectual Property Violations**:
   - Copying or cloning Faheemly code without authorization
   - Removing copyright notices or attribution
   - Encouraging others to infringe IP rights
   - Creating unauthorized derivatives

4. **Malicious Activities**:
   - Deliberately introducing security vulnerabilities
   - Denial-of-service attacks
   - Unauthorized access attempts
   - Reverse engineering for competitive purposes

### Reporting:

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it:

üìß **conduct@faheemly.com**

Include:

All reports will be handled with discretion. We will:
- Keep reporter identity confidential
- Investigate promptly and fairly
- Take appropriate action

| Stage | Timeline |
|-------|----------|
| Acknowledgment | Within 24 hours |
| Investigation | Within 7 days |
| Action | Within 14 days |
| Follow-up | As needed |

## ‚öñÔ∏è Consequences

Violators may face:

1. **Warning**: Private written warning explaining violation
2. **Temporary Ban**: Suspension from community for specified period
3. **Permanent Ban**: Indefinite exclusion from community
4. **Legal Action**: For IP violations or illegal activities

### Escalation:

- **First Offense**: Warning (minor violations)
- **Second Offense**: Temporary ban (1-30 days)
- **Third Offense**: Permanent ban
- **Severe Violations**: Immediate permanent ban + legal action
