## üìú Legal Framework

### Copyright Law:

- Protects source code as "literary work"
- Infringement: copying, distribution, derivative works
- Penalties: Up to $150,000 per work + criminal charges

### Patent Law:

- Protects novel inventions and methods
- Infringement: Using patented methods without license
- Penalties: Injunctions, damages, attorney's fees

### Trade Secret Law:

- Protects confidential business information
- Infringement: Misappropriation, theft, espionage
- Penalties: Treble damages, criminal prosecution
